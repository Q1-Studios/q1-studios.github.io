About us

We're a small team of passionate computer science students building games in their free time.

Seal
Coding Art
50% 3D Artist, 50% Mad Scientist, 100% Seal. Boasting natural buoyancy and a skill set in 3D Art that rivals Leonardo da Vinci (if da Vinci was a god-tier shitposter), Seal never backs down from tackling a challenging project. At Q1, he's a force to be reckoned with, pushing both visuals and collateral damage to their maximum.

Cucu is a botanical wonder, running entirely on spite and way too little sleep. When he gets excited about something, nothing except for the laws of physics (i.e. hitting him with a brick) can stop him.
